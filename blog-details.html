How Modern Adhesive Technology is Transforming Manufacturing in India

By Teraxo Adhesives
February 8, 2025

Over the last decade, India’s manufacturing industry has undergone a rapid transformation. With increasing demand for faster production, stronger bonding, and cleaner finishing, adhesive technology has become a crucial part of industries including footwear, furniture, signage, automotive, and interior fabrication.

At Teraxo, we have closely worked with manufacturers to develop high-performance cyanoacrylate adhesives that reduce curing time, improve product quality, and support large-scale production efficiency. Our instant bonding solutions are engineered to meet real-world challenges across multiple industries.

“In today’s fast-moving manufacturing environment, the right adhesive can reduce production time by up to 50% and significantly improve product durability.”

Teraxo Technical Team

The Rise of Instant Adhesives in Indian Manufacturing

Traditional bonding methods—such as screws, nails, and slow-curing glues—are no longer practical for modern production lines. Industries now require clean bonding, fast application, and strong adhesion within seconds. This is where cyanoacrylate adhesives like FX40, R40, and W40 have revolutionized workflow.

Why Cyanoacrylate Adhesives Are the Future

Instant adhesives offer unmatched speed and versatility. Whether it’s bonding wood, PVC, acrylic, rubber, leather, metal, or stone, CA adhesives provide reliable performance with a clean, professional finish. This makes them perfect for industries where precision, speed, and strength are essential.

  • Fast curing time reduces production delays
  • Extremely strong bonding performance
  • Compatible with multi-material manufacturing

As customer expectations rise and manufacturing continues to evolve, industries are shifting towards adhesives that provide efficiency, consistency, and cost-effective performance. Teraxo is committed to supporting this transformation with high-quality products and industry-specific adhesive solutions.
