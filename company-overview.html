About Us

A Trusted Name in Instant Adhesive Solutions

Teraxo Adhesives delivers high-performance cyanoacrylate solutions engineered for industries that demand speed, strength, and reliability — from footwear and interiors to signage, woodworking, automotive, PVC/UPVC fabrication, and hardware applications.

Teraxo Adhesives was founded with a simple goal — to offer industrial-grade instant adhesives that combine fast curing with strong, reliable, and clean bonding. Since our beginning, we have grown into a trusted B2B supplier serving manufacturers, wholesalers, and OEM brands across India.

  • Consistent bonding performance
  • Trusted by multiple industries

Our mission is to manufacture high-quality, fast-curing adhesives that help industries increase productivity, achieve superior finishing, and build durable products with confidence.

  • Deliver strong, long-lasting bonds
  • Support businesses with premium solutions

Our vision is to become one of India’s most trusted adhesive brands, known for innovation, quality, and customer-centric solutions, while expanding our manufacturing and distribution network nationwide.

  • Innovation-driven formulations
  • Expanding to PAN India markets
