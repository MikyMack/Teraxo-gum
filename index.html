Why Choose Teraxo

Stronger Bonds. Faster Work. Trusted Results.

Teraxo Adhesives are engineered for fast curing, strong bonding, and reliable performance across multiple materials including wood, PVC, rubber, metal, acrylic, and more.

  • Industrial-grade bonding strength
  • Clean, fast, and consistent curing
  • Zero whitening & premium finish options

Our adhesive range is designed for the specific needs of industries like footwear, signage, interiors, woodworking, PVC/UPVC fabrication, automotive, handicrafts, and hardware retail.

  • Tailored formulations for every sector
  • High productivity for manufacturing units
  • Reliable bonding for diverse materials

Every Teraxo product goes through strict quality testing to ensure fast curing, stable viscosity, long-lasting bonds, and maximum reliability for industrial users.

  • Batch-wise quality checks
  • 6–12 month shelf life stability
  • Trusted by manufacturers & wholesalers
