Non-Toxic Adhesives for Handicrafts & Toys Manufacturing

Teraxo FX40 and child-safe adhesives are specially formulated for handicrafts manufacturing, toy assembly, decorative items, and artisanal products. Our non-toxic, EN71-certified formulas ensure safety while providing strong, durable bonds for creative applications.

In handicrafts and toys manufacturing, adhesives must meet stringent safety standards while bonding diverse materials from wood to fabric to plastic. Teraxo's specialized formulations provide reliable bonding without compromising on safety, ensuring that finished products are durable, beautiful, and safe for children and consumers alike.

Common Challenges in Handicrafts & Toys Manufacturing

Safety Concerns

Standard adhesives may contain toxic chemicals unsuitable for children's products.

Material Diversity

Need to bond wood, fabric, paper, plastic, metal, and ceramics in single products.

Regulatory Compliance

Meeting international safety standards like EN71, ASTM F963, and CPSIA requirements.

Moisture Sensitivity

Handicrafts exposed to humidity or occasional cleaning require moisture-resistant bonds.

Temperature Effects

Seasonal temperature changes can cause expansion/contraction leading to bond failure.

Production Speed

Slow-curing adhesives delay production in small workshops and handicraft units.
