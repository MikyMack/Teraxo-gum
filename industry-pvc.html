High-Performance Adhesives for PVC & UPVC Fabrication

Teraxo W40 and PVC-specific adhesives are engineered for the unique challenges of plastic fabrication. Our waterproof, chemical-resistant formulas provide strong, durable bonds for window manufacturing, pipe joining, profile assembly, and plastic fabrication applications.

In PVC and UPVC fabrication, adhesives must bond low-surface-energy plastics, withstand weather exposure, and resist plasticizer migration. Teraxo's specialized adhesives are formulated specifically for PVC and UPVC materials, providing reliable bonds that maintain integrity in outdoor applications, plumbing systems, and structural plastic assemblies.

Common Challenges in PVC & UPVC Fabrication

Waterproof Bonding

Standard adhesives fail in wet environments, especially for plumbing and outdoor applications.

UV Degradation

Outdoor PVC applications require UV-resistant adhesives that won't yellow or weaken.

Plasticizer Migration

PVC plasticizers can migrate and weaken standard adhesive bonds over time.

Temperature Cycling

PVC expansion and contraction with temperature changes can break weak bonds.

Low Surface Energy

PVC's low surface energy makes it difficult for standard adhesives to wet and bond properly.

Slow Production

Long curing times slow down window manufacturing and pipe fabrication processes.

How Teraxo Solves PVC Fabrication Challenges

Waterproof Bonds

W40 creates permanent waterproof bonds ideal for plumbing, outdoor windows, and wet environments.

UV Resistant

Formulated to resist yellowing and degradation from prolonged sun exposure in outdoor applications.

Plasticizer Tolerant

Special formulation resists plasticizer migration that weakens bonds in flexible PVC applications.

Flexible Bonds

Maintains bond integrity through PVC expansion and contraction with temperature changes.

Fast Production

3-10 second curing enables faster assembly lines in window manufacturing and pipe fabrication.

Chemical Resistance

Withstands exposure to cleaning chemicals, pool chlorine, and other harsh substances.

PVC & UPVC Applications

Window & Door Manufacturing

Bonding PVC profiles, corner joints, reinforcement inserts, and hardware attachments.

Pipe & Fitting Assembly

Joining PVC pipes, fittings, valves, and creating waterproof seals in plumbing systems.

Sheet Fabrication

Bonding PVC sheets for tanks, ducting, chemical containers, and industrial applications.

Profile Extrusion Assembly

Assembling extruded PVC profiles for furniture, trim, edging, and decorative elements.

Signage & Display

Bonding PVC letters, display boards, exhibition stands, and indoor/outdoor signage.

Building Products

Assembling PVC cladding, skirting boards, cable management, and electrical conduits.
