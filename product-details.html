FX40 – Instant Adhesive (Premium Grade)

FX40 is Teraxo’s premium instant adhesive engineered for sign-making, PVC/UPVC, acrylic and other display applications where a clean, non-whitening finish is essential.

FX40 is a fast-curing ethyl cyanoacrylate adhesive formulated to deliver instant grip, high bond strength and a crystal-clear finish. Its advanced anti-whitening formula prevents bloom/whitening on glossy, matte and textured surfaces—making it ideal for signage, acrylic fabrication and interior display work.

  • Chemical Base: Ethyl Cyanoacrylate
  • Appearance: Clear Liquid (Anti-Whitening)
  • Shelf Life: 6 Months
  • Available Packs: 20 g (box), 40 g, 50 g
  • Cure Time: 1–3 seconds (typical)

Key Features

  • Zero whitening / zero blooming
  • Ultra-fast initial grab (1–3s)
  • Clean, clear finish on glossy & textured sheets
  • Excellent for large surface bonding & sign fabrication
  • Works on acrylic, PVC, UPVC, flex, marble & more

FX40 is commonly used by signage fabricators and interior contractors where appearance matters. It cures rapidly—reducing assembly time—and gives a stable, long-lasting bond under typical indoor conditions.

Application Tips

For best results: clean & dry surfaces, apply a thin bead of FX40, align parts and hold for 5–10 seconds. Use AS40 activator for instant curing on porous substrates or in very low humidity conditions.
