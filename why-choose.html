Industries We Serve

Footwear Manufacturing

Attaching soles, straps, and upper components with fast curing adhesives for high-speed production lines.

Signage & Flex Board

Acrylic sheet joining, PVC letters, flex board assembly with non-whitening adhesives for clean finishes.

Furniture & Woodworking

Wood crack filling, decorative work, furniture repair and reinforcement with specialized wood-grade adhesives.

Interiors & Modular Kitchen

PVC/UPVC panel fixing, acrylic sheet pasting, edge trims and mouldings for on-site installation.

Automotive Aftermarket

Plastic clips & trims, dashboard bonding, rubber beading fixing for precision repairs in workshops.

PVC & UPVC Fabrication

Packaging Industry
